Elftime Stories Under the Northern Lights

Don't you miss the times of great storytelling? Join the Elves in their favorite evening activity - reading Christmas stories!

Main

Highlights

Journey through the Arctic Forest for story time by the cozy fire. Take a break from your phone and devices, and let your mind rest as you snuggle under a warm blanket with a snack and listen to the Elf tale of your own choosing. And who knows - as the fire grows warmer and the Northern Lights begin their dance, you may just come to realize that all those stories you loved as a child are not just fairy tales after all.

Included

  • Welcoming by the Elf

  • Fairy tale walk in the snowy forest
  • Hot drinks by the open log fire
  • Making sausages on the fireplace
  • A shortened Christmas story of your own choosing (5 different stories available)
  • Return transfer
  • Opportunity to take as many pictures with own camera as desired
  • Opportunity to provide advance information about your nearest and dearest to make the experience even more special
Price:
  • 89€ / adult; 69 € / child (2-12 years old); free for children under 2 years old
